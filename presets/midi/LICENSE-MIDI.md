The MIDI files in this directory are from the Groove MIDI Dataset
(Magenta, Google Research), licensed under Creative Commons Attribution
4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

Citation:
  Gillick, J., Roberts, A., Engel, J., Eck, D., & Bamman, D.
  (2019). Learning to Groove with Inverse Sequence Transformations.
  International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML).
  https://magenta.tensorflow.org/datasets/groove

Original dataset:
  https://storage.googleapis.com/magentadata/datasets/groove/groove-v1.0.0-midionly.zip

License text:
  https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode

The tracks in this directory have been trimmed to ~20-40 seconds (8-16 bars
of musical content, plus a couple of short fills) and renamed by
style/BPM/beat-type for clarity. No notes, velocities, or tempo events have
been altered; only trailing material past the cutoff was removed.
